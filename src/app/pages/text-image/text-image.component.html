Meet Danish Invoice Standards with BlueSeries XML

In Denmark, OIOXML format is required for all invoices sent to government agencies and institutions, with fixed requirements for the information included in the invoice. BlueSeries XML can process invoices from spool files and convert them into OIOXML format for sending via email or FTP to the appropriate recipient. Additionally, a style sheet can be attached to ensure proper graphic display.
